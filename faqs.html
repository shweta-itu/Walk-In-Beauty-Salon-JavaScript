FAQs

Before and After Hours:
We do take special requests for services before and after hours. Additionally, we will gladly accommodate your group for a private party in the spa. Please call for reservations.
Salon and Spa Guidelines:
We encourage you to arrive 30 minutes before your treatment time to relax and enjoy our steam, sauna, and relaxation room. For your comfort, we suggest you shower before all massages. Arriving late will limit the time for our treatment, thus lessening its effectiveness and your enjoyment. Your treatment will end on time so the next guest is not delayed. As a courtesy to other guests and our therapists, please inform us if you must cancel or reschedule. The full value of your treatment will be charged if you do not inform us in advance that you are unable to make your appointment. Lockers, robes, and slippers are provided for all of our guests.
Cancellation Policy:
All services are reserved especially for you. To avoid a full fee charge, we require 24 hours notice of any cancellations. A valid credit card is required to hold your reservation.
