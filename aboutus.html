About Us

Walk-In has a Diploma in cosmetology from Woman's World Cosmetology University from San Josa, USA. Also, she has a license holder and certified trainer from California State. She has over 25 years of experience in the Hair beauty parlour businesses.

She has expanded her techniques by developing her own innovative system for Threading, Facial, Waxing and hair design. She is a down to earth artisan who is genuinely devoted to her work.
